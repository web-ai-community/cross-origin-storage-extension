Cross-Origin Storage

Options

⚠️ Enabling this may break some sites. Bot-detection systems such as Cloudflare Turnstile inspect the Worker and SharedWorker globals; patching them can trigger false positives.

If enabled, workers spawned by pages will have access to the COS API (navigator.crossOriginStorage).

The Public Hash List (PHL) is a curated allowlist of resource hashes widely deployed enough that their presence in a shared cache reveals nothing specific about your browsing history. When enabled, a requested hash that isn't on the PHL is treated as unavailable — and the access is blocked and logged — even if it's actually present in the local COS cache. Off by default; the PHL is fetched from GitHub and refreshed at most once every 24 hours.
